Why is it important to teach Emotional Intelligence in High School?

It is necessary to teach emotional intelligence in High school because emotional intelligence is one of the factors that lead decision making and sometimes emotions can lead people to make the wrong decision that could ruin their future on one choice.Emotional intelligence teaching in high will have the greatest impact due to the cause that in this stage is where adolescents have more emotions and have to deal with responsibilities,stress,drugs,harder classes and many more difficulties than can lead to negative emotions such as depression,anxiety,no self esteem and sadness all of these emotions can sometimes take over the life of the person and make it miserable but if they were teached how to fight and control the negative emotions they will not fall into drug consumption.People with a higher Emotional intelligence tend to do better in jobs and in social life because they have high self esteem and understand theirs and others emotions that makes them great leaders,friend and persons.
